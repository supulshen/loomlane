Our Story

Loom & Lane was founded with a vision to celebrate Sri Lankan craftsmanship and bring authentic, handcrafted lifestyle products to discerning customers around the world.

Born from a passion for traditional artistry and sustainable living, we work directly with skilled artisans across Sri Lanka to curate a unique collection of home décor, wooden crafts, porcelain items, and decorative accessories.

Each product tells a story of heritage, dedication, and the timeless beauty of handmade craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote traditional Sri Lankan craftsmanship while empowering local artisans and communities.

We are committed to:

  • Supporting fair trade and ethical sourcing practices
  • Promoting sustainable and eco-friendly products
  • Preserving traditional craft techniques for future generations
  • Providing authentic, high-quality lifestyle products
  • Building meaningful connections between artisans and customers

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

Every product is genuinely handcrafted by skilled Sri Lankan artisans using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and sustainable production methods that respect both people and the planet.

Quality

We maintain the highest standards of craftsmanship and carefully curate each piece to ensure exceptional quality.
