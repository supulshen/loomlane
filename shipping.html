Shipping Policy

Home Shipping Policy

At Loom & Lane, we are committed to delivering your handcrafted products safely and efficiently. Please review our shipping policy to understand our delivery options, costs, and timelines.

Delivery Options

Within Sri Lanka
  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days (LKR 350)
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (LKR 750)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for Colombo area only (LKR 1,500)
  • Free Shipping: On orders over LKR 10,000
International Shipping
  • Asia: 5-10 business days (starting from LKR 2,500)
  • Europe: 7-14 business days (starting from LKR 3,500)
  • North America: 7-14 business days (starting from LKR 4,000)
  • Rest of World: 10-21 business days (starting from LKR 4,500)

Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Saturday, excluding public holidays). Processing time may be longer during peak seasons or promotional periods.

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM are usually processed on the same day
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends will be processed on the next business day
  • Custom or made-to-order items may require 5-7 business days for processing

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • A shipping confirmation email with tracking number
  • Real-time tracking updates via SMS (for domestic orders)
  • Delivery notifications

You can track your order by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your confirmation email
  • Logging into your account and viewing order history
  • Contacting our customer service team

Delivery Areas

Domestic Shipping (Sri Lanka)

We deliver to all areas within Sri Lanka, including:

  • Colombo & Suburbs
  • Galle
  • Kandy
  • Kalutara
  • Anuradhapura
  • Jaffna
  • Negombo
  • Matara
  • Nuwara Eliya
  • All other districts
International Shipping

We currently ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Please contact us if your country is not listed at checkout.

Packaging & Care

All our products are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Eco-friendly packaging materials
  • Bubble wrap and padding for fragile items
  • Secure boxes to prevent damage during transit
  • Weather-resistant outer packaging for international shipments

Customs & Import Duties

For international orders:

  • Customers are responsible for all import duties, taxes, and customs fees
  • These charges are not included in our product prices or shipping costs
  • Customs policies vary by country; please check with your local customs office
  • Refusal to pay customs charges may result in the package being returned to sender

Shipping Delays

While we strive to meet our delivery estimates, delays may occur due to:

  • Public holidays
  • Weather conditions
  • Customs clearance (for international orders)
  • Incorrect shipping address
  • Peak season demand

If your order is significantly delayed, please contact us for assistance.
